About Basti Ki Pathshala

We are more than just an organization — we are a movement dedicated to educating and empowering children in slums through compassionate volunteer efforts and grassroots action.

By providing access to education, we aim to break the cycle of poverty and uplift entire communities. Every child deserves a chance to learn, grow, and dream big.
